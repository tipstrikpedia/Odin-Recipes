Chicken Satay Recipe

Description

Chicken satay is grilled chicken skewers served with peanut sauce. It is popular in Indonesia and has a sweet, savory, and smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken breast or thigh, cut into smal pieces
  • Bamboo skewers
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp limejuice

Peanut sauce:

  • 150 g roasted peanuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 red chilies
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 150 ml water

Steps

  1. Soak bamboo skewers in water for 15 minutes
  2. Put chicken pieces onto the skewers
  3. mix sweet soy sauce, cooking oil, and lime juice. Brush it onto the chicken
  4. Grill the chicken until cooked and slightly browned
  5. Blend roasted peanuts, garlic, chilies, salt, sugar, and water until smooth
  6. Cook the peanut sauce in a pan for a few minutes. Add sweet soy sauceand stir well
  7. Serve the chicken satay with peanut sauce
