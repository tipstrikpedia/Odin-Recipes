Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried ricce is a simple Indonesian dish made with rice, eggs, vegetables, and sweet soy sauce. it is easy to cook and suitable for breakfast, launch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 plates cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 3 shallots, chopped
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Optional: chicken, shrimp, or vegetables

Steps

  1. Healt oil in a pan.
  2. saute garlic and shallots until fragrant
  3. Add eggs and stir until cooked
  4. Add chicken, shrimp, or vegetables if using. Cook for a few minutes
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  6. Add sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir evently
  7. Add chopped green onions and cook for another minute.
  8. Serve warm with cucumber, tomato, or fried egg
